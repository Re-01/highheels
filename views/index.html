Founded in 1972 by entrepreneurial brothers Anderson and Jefferson Birman, today Arezzo is the largest retailer of women's footwear in Latin America, bringing together concept, high quality and contemporary design.

The consolidation of the brand in the domestic market can also be seen from its history of awards: it seven times Best Franchise in Brazil in the Clothing, Footwear and Accessories Segment (Small Business & Big Business Magazine); Alshop Visa Award, also received the HorsConcours Award by the consumer vote; eight years of Excellence in Franchising (Brazilian Franchising Association), Top of Mind (La Clé) and is not in the ranking of the Most Valuable Brands in Brazil (Isto É Dinheiro).

Today, the Arezzo franchise chain has more than 356 stores in 180 municipalities and is present in all Brazilian states. Choose your favorite Arezzo, we are waiting for you.
